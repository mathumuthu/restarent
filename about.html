About Grand House

A legacy of culinary excellence since 1985

Our Heritage

A Story of Passion & Excellence

Founded in 1985 by Chef Antonio Rossi, Grand House began as a small family trattoria in the heart of St. Moritz. What started as a humble establishment with just six tables has evolved into one of Switzerland's most celebrated fine dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: to create extraordinary culinary experiences that honor both tradition and innovation. Every dish tells a story, every ingredient is carefully sourced, and every guest becomes part of our extended family.

"Cooking is not just about ingredients and recipes. It's about creating memories that last a lifetime."

Journey

Our History

1985

The Beginning

Chef Antonio Rossi opens the first Grand House restaurant with a vision to bring authentic Italian cuisine to St. Moritz.

1995

First Michelin Star

Grand House earns its first Michelin star, establishing itself as a culinary destination in Switzerland.

2008

Expansion & Renovation

A complete renovation of the historic building while preserving its Alpine charm and character.

2015

Third Michelin Star

Achieving the highest culinary honor, joining the elite ranks of three-Michelin-starred restaurants.

2024

New Generation

Chef Maria Rossi takes the helm, bringing fresh innovation while honoring her father's legacy.
