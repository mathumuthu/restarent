FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner service and weekends. You can book online through our reservation page or call us directly.

What is your dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. We ask that guests refrain from wearing sportswear, beachwear, or flip-flops. Jackets are recommended but not required for gentlemen during dinner service.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We cater to various dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergies. Please inform us when making your reservation so our chefs can prepare accordingly.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all dinner guests. There is also a public parking garage within a 2-minute walk from the restaurant.

Can I host a private event?

Yes, we have private dining rooms available for events ranging from intimate gatherings to corporate functions. Contact our events team at events@grandhouse.com for customized packages.
